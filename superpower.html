What is Super-speed?

Super-speed is the ability to run very fast at high speeds and maybe go back in time according to the law of physics if ran fast enough. The person can move much faster than the average member of their species, some near or at the speed of sound, or even faster. However, this power is not without any ill effects, as it can strain the body, although some users may be resistant or even immune to the effects of high speed travel, including friction, g-force etc. In other words Super-speed can be very dangerous in situations, according to speed related to biology the human body can be injured severely due to mass of g-force, no oxygen, and many more cases that can be related to life or death situations. When a user gains the ability to have Super-speed it should be used with much precaution.
